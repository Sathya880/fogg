About Us

Fogg is a popular Indian perfume and deodorant brand known for its affordable yet long-lasting fragrances. They've gained a significant market share in India, offering a wide range of products catering to both men and women. Fogg is particularly famous for its innovative packaging and strong marketing campaigns emphasizing its long-lasting fragrance technology. If you're interested in creating a commercial for them, focusing on dynamic video shots could really capture their energetic brand essence!
